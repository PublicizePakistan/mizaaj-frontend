Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Your privacy matters to us. Mizaj collects only what is necessary to deliver your personality report and never sells your personal data to third parties.

Information We Collect

When you create an account and take the Mizaj personality test, we collect the following information:

  • Full name and email address
  • Date of birth, time of birth, and place of birth (used for astrological context in your report)
  • Your answers to the 60-question personality assessment
  • Payment information processed securely through Dialog Pay

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as national identity numbers, medical records, or biometric data.

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Generating and storing your personality type report
  • Processing your one-time payment securely
  • Allowing you to retrieve your report upon future login
  • Improving the accuracy and quality of the Mizaj assessment

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the services described above.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in a secure database hosted on industry-standard cloud infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. Passwords are hashed and are never stored in plain text.

Payment transactions are handled entirely by our payment provider. Mizaj does not store your card details, bank account information, or any other financial credentials.

Data Sharing

Mizaj does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Our payment provider — for processing your payment securely
  • Our cloud hosting provider — for running our platform infrastructure
  • Legal obligations — if required by Pakistani law or a valid court order

Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. To do so, contact us at the email below. We will process your request within 7 business days.

You may also request a copy of all data we hold about you by contacting us directly.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us at info@organicles.pk or visit our Contact page.
